Profile

My specialties are user research and analysis, visual design, and digital and hand-drawn illustrations. I am looking for a position that lets me use all of my specialties creatively. I am very passionate about art, creativity, and Human-Computer Interaction, and I want to put those passions to work for the right industry.

Experience

Embedded myself in the psychedelic and mental health industries in order to better understand users and stakeholders in this emerging and misunderstood market. Attended conferences and conducted multiple user interviews to generate personas and gather requirements.
